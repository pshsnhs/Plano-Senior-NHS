What is an Initiative?

An iniative is a service opportunity YOU find yourself out in the community!

It cannot be a service done or closely affiliated within Plano Senior High School but can be within PISD!

Initiative Approval Form

When you have found an initiative source, click on the link below and fill out the google form.

YOU MUST FILL OUT THE FORM AT LEAST THREE DAYS BEFORE YOU ACTUALLY GO AND VOLUNTEER!!! (Anything sooner will NOT get approved)

Every member must complete the google form, even if another member has already been approved for the same initiative.

We'll try to get back to you within 1-2 days :)

Initiative Email Template

Below is a link to email templates that you must use when emailing your initiative source sponsor.

Make sure to have them email their template to planoseniorhsnhs@gmail.com (NOT back to you)!
